Top 5 Smartphones

iPhone 13

The iPhone 13 comes with a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, A15 Bionic chip, dual 12MP cameras, and up to 512GB of storage.

Samsung Galaxy S21

The Samsung Galaxy S21 comes with a 6.2-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, Exynos 2100 or Snapdragon 888 chipset, triple 12MP cameras, and up to 256GB of storage.

Google Pixel 6

The Google Pixel 6 comes with a 6.4-inch AMOLED display, Google Tensor chip, dual 50MP cameras, and up to 256GB of storage.

OnePlus 9 Pro

The OnePlus 9 Pro comes with a 6.7-inch Fluid AMOLED display, Snapdragon 888 chipset, quad 48MP cameras, and up to 256GB of storage.

Xiaomi Mi 11

The Xiaomi Mi 11 comes with a 6.81-inch AMOLED display, Snapdragon 888 chipset, triple 108MP cameras, and up to 256GB of storage.
